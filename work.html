SHIVAN & NARRESH

Art Director & Asst. Print Designer

SHIVAN & NARRESH is India’s first luxury holiday brand that was launched in Cannes at ‘Mare di Moda’ with its 100% stitch-free swim line. The brand has a unique signature style that is bold, confident & sophisticated. With a keen focus on holiday lifestyles, the brand’s offering encompasses the modern wardrobe of holiday connoisseurs. The new collection 'foxfires" is based on abstract themed art prints and the one of the blue coloured prints has been exclusively crafted for Walkers & Co at Cannes Film Festival.
